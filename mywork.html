Games

Knockout Arena
I convinced my teacher to let me solo develop for a semester-long game project, and this is the result of 2 months of work so far, all done between 16 credits and a part time job! This FPS PvE Arena Shooter is the combination of two game ideas that I've had for a while, and it is proof of my design and development skills. I pushed myself in many different aspects for this project, having never before worked with AI navigation, ragdoll physics (to this extent), mapmaking (used ProBuilder for the first time, although I have made plenty of Portal 2 levels), and more.

You can find a link to the itch.io page [PC build] here.

Color of Darkness
This is a game where the objective is simple: survive. All you have to do is move around in this 2D auto-attacking game. Upgrade different weapons and use powerups to fight the constant horde of enemies coming your way! I was the lead programmer and audio designer, creating the logic for the character controller, powerups, and all of the game audio/music. This game was created in Unity and C# with a 5-week scope in mind.

You can play the game in your browser [WebGL build] here.
Cloud Chow
Play as a dragon in this infinite 2D side-scroller, trying to eat as many clouds as you can before you inevitably lose! The game will slowly ramp up in diffuclty, as the speed increases and so does the spawn rate/damage of the storm clouds! Earn the highest score you can! I was the responsible for collision handling, audio design, and UI for development of the game. This game was created in Unity and C# with a 4-week scope in mind.

You can play the game in your browser [WebGL build] here.
Timeless Torture
You wake up in the wrong time period with evil closing in. Find the haunted items around the house and before time runs out. After all items are found and burned in the fireplace with the evil cleansed, a portal will appear, which you have to find to travel to the next time period. I was the game lead and lead programmer, managing the team and programming nearly everything. It was created in MonoGame and C#, which was new to me at the time.

You can find a link to the itch.io page [PC build] here.
Flappy Flippers
This is a 2D side-scroller phone game where the objective is to get the highest score you can by flying through the icicles with the penguin! Penguins can't actually fly, of course, which is why it has a jetpack strapped to its back! I worked on this solo between classes for a few weeks during my first semester of college to practice Unity and to challenge myself to do my own art for once.

Class of 21XX
“Class of 21XX” is a puzzle/skill 2D platformer that follows four friends about to graduate from Faith Academy, a school for future space cadets. An unexpected alien attack puts their test on hold, forcing them to save their school, fix the ship, and go out with one last hurrah. This was created during a 2 week game jam at the USC Summer Program, and I was the lead programmer who had to learn Unity and C#, BOTH for the first time.
